Chicken curry

Ingredient Amount
Chicken breast 700gr
Yellow onion 1 cup chopped
Olive oil 2 Tbsp
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Ginger 1 Tbsp, minced
Chicken broth 1 cup
Tomatoes 3/4 cup
Heavy cream 1/3 cup
Cilantro 2 Tbsp
  1. Step 1: Saute onion, garlic and ginger in a skillet
  2. Step 2: Add broth and tomatoes
  3. Step 3: Blend mixture in a blender as directed then return to skillet
  4. Step 4: Season sauce then add chicken
  5. Step 5: Simmer chicken in sauce until cooked through
  6. Step 6: Stir in cream
  7. Step 7: Serve over rice garnished with cilantro
